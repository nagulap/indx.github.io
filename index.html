    Who are we?

    Based in Silicon Valley, we are a highly motivated team of Entrepreneurs, Doctors, Engineers and Data Scientists with the mission to develop innovative software products, help our collaborators find a Cure-for-Cancer and make a societal impact, globally.Founded by experienced management team with proven track record of founding 5 companies, raising over $50M in investment and creating over $500M of exit value.

    Problem we are solving

    Data silos notoriously encumber life science and health care industry. Researchers and Clinicians do not have an easy access to clinical data, lab data, genomic data and medical images etc. We believe that having the information accessible at their fingertips (anywhere, anytime) and leveraging the analytical capability of the computers, we can transform this industry.

    Our Solutions

    Our goal is to solve this very unaddressed problem, connect the disconnected data silos, break down the walls and artificial barriers, integrate all the information and provide a “single patient view (SPV)” so that researchers and clinicians can take the critical decisions more efficiently, enabling Better Outcomes and improved Patient Care.Indx has developed a state of the art informatics platform called iCore that facilitate data aggregation, abstraction, analysis, visualization and delivery from across the continuum of care (P2P: Patient, Physician, Pathologist, Payers). It’s a cloud based, mobile friendly, HIPAA compliant architecture and responsive design allows rapid development of innovative applications that can be deployed and supported globally.
